Environmental management – guidance

Apply for a standard rules waste permit

From: Environment Agency and Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs

First published: 6 April 2016

Part of: Flooding and coastal change, Environmental permits and Flooding and coastal change

Applies to: England

Find out if your activity could be covered by a standard rules waste permit, and how to apply.

Contents

  1. Before you apply
  2. How to apply
  3. Other ways to apply

Before you apply

We recommend that you contact us for a pre-application discussion.

Order a nature and heritage screening report to check that your site meets the location conditions for the permit. You don’t need this for mobile plant permits.

Read the rules in the standard rules waste permits to find out whether your activity is covered by standard rules.

If you can't meet the conditions for standard rules you will need to apply for a bespoke permit.

How to apply

When you select a permit during the application, we'll tell you what documents you need and what you need to do to apply.
