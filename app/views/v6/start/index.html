How to apply

Read the rules in the standard rules waste permits to find out whether your activity is covered by standard rules.

If you can't meet the conditions for standard rules you will need to apply for a bespoke permit.

Apply for a standard rules waste permit online

Before you start

When you select a permit during the application, we'll tell you what documents you need and what you need to do to apply.
