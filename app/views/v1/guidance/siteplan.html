Site plan

The site plan must include:

  • all of the land on which your operations take place
  • surrounding local features to help us locate it
  • a clearly-marked outline of the site
  • the date it was prepared
  • a scale

For water discharges it must also show the:

  • sewage treatment plant
  • sample point
  • point where the sewage is discharged

The plan must be legible at A4 size and drawn accurately to scale.

Scan or save the plan as a JPG, TIFF or PDF file. Maximum file size is 10MB. You can upload up to 5 plans.
